# Privacy Policy for Xlens

**Last updated:** January 22, 2026

## Introduction
Xlens ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles your information.

## Data Collection and Usage
**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

*   **Image Processing:** all image processing and Optical Character Recognition (OCR) is performed entirely **offline** on your device using Google ML Kit. No images are uploaded to any server.
*   **Storage Access:** The app requires access to your device's camera and storage solely for the purpose of capturing images and saving extracted text/PDF files. This data stays on your device.

## Third-Party Services
We use **Google ML Kit** for on-device text recognition. Google ML Kit processing happens locally on your device.
*   [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## Permissions
To function, Xlens requires the following permissions:
*   **Camera:** To capture documents for scanning.
*   **Storage (Read/Write):** To import images from your gallery and save extracted text/PDF files.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
